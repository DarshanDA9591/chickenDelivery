About us
"Welcome to Chicken Delivery, your trusted online destination for premium chicken, seafood, and eggs, conveniently delivered to your doorstep in Bangalore. We offer a wide range of fresh, high-quality products sourced from local farms and suppliers, ensuring the best taste and hygiene. Whether you’re preparing a family meal or planning a special dish, our carefully selected products promise top-notch quality and freshness. At Chicken Delivery, we’re committed to bringing you convenience, reliability, and superior service, all while supporting sustainable practices in the food industry."
Fresh Chicken

"Discover our range of premium, free-range chicken that is hormone-free and sourced from trusted local farms. Whether you're looking for tender broiler chicken or the rich flavor of country (Nati) chicken, we have something for every taste. Sea Foods
